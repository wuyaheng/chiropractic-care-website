About Dr. Dureja

Dr. Neeti Dureja is committed to offering her patients the latest, most advanced treatment options in conjunction with effective chiropractic care. She uses the ground-breaking new MLS Laser Therapy, a transformative technology that relieves pain and stimulates healing of various issues, from neuropathy to sciatica to neck pain. Dr. Dureja also specializes in weight loss and educating patients on nutritional supplementation. Additionally, she loves improving the lives of elderly clients and has worked alongside medical doctors at various nursing homes, treating a variety of disorders using chiropractic methods, physical therapy, rehabilitation, and nutrition.

Dr. Dureja earned her Doctor or Chiropractic degree from the University of Bridgeport in Connecticut along with a postgraduate degree in Clinical Nutrition and an undergraduate degree from Stony Brook University. She is also an active member of the Nassau County Medical Reserve Corporation and the American Chiropractic Association.
